Overview

Purpose

Hi, my name is Ty Wittmann and I am a web design student. I would like to make a blog type website to help the homeless communities in north Idaho. I want this website to contain useful information regarding homeless resources as well as allow people to post opportunities they have, regarding living space and work, in order to help the homeless community. This helps inform this community, as well as give a direct connection between those willing to help and those who need help the most. This website will help improve the lives of those less fortunate and help improve communities overall.

Audience

This website is intended for those in the north idaho community who are in homeless situation to find helpful reasources, as well as others in the community who are willing to help that can post opportunities regarding living space, work, or food.
